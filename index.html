// 01 — About

What I do

I'm a Technical Support Engineer with five years at a SaaS document management company, where I specialize in integration troubleshooting, SSO/SAML issues, and document automation apps.

Most of my time is spent at the integration layer — tracing API calls with Fiddler and Wireshark, reading HAR captures, and working backward from a broken 401 or 500 to find the actual root cause before escalating to Engineering.

I've also been building AI-assisted support workflows since before it became a talking point — including a Claude-based toolset for our customer service team, with the director's backing, to help people work through tickets faster.
